üöÄQuick Start Guide

1. Add Media: Click the "Add Video", "Add Image", or "Add Audio" buttons to import your files.
2. Add Text: Click "Add Text" and type your message. Use the alignment buttons to position it.
3. Edit Clips: Double-click any clip on the timeline to adjust its timing, position, or properties.
4. Preview: Hit the Play button to see your creation in action!
5. Save: Click "Save Project" to save your project as a JSON file. The file are saved in your default Downloads folder. (Note : Media files (Images, video or audio) are not saved in project file, you need to re-add them after opening an existing project.)
6. Export: Click "Export Video" when you're ready. The magic happens in your browser! Video file are saved in your default Downloads folder.

üí°Pro Tips

  • Drag clips on the timeline to change their start time
  • Click and drag the red cursor to see different parts of the video.
  • Double click the clips or elements to change their settings.
  • Use the zoom controls to get a better view of your timeline
  • Click "Zoom to Fit" to see your entire project at once
  • Save frequently to avoid losing your work!
  • The timeline auto-adjusts to fit all your content
  • To cut out a video or audio clip somewhere in the middle, add the same clip twice and set the start and end times on both.
